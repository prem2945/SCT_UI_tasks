Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew & Bean started as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a big dream: to bring exceptional coffee to our community. What began as a passion project has grown into a beloved local institution.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, roast them in-house daily, and craft each cup with care. Our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability drives everything we do.
